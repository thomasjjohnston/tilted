Full App Mockups

Twenty-four screens covering every flow in the MVP spec, rendered in the Classic Premium direction. Paired with design tokens and a component inventory to hand directly to the development team. Content is realistic, not Lorem Ipsum — engineers should be able to read chip math, action logs, and copy as-is.
