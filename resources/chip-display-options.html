Tilted · Chip Display · 10 Options

Stack Representation

The current design shows "Total" as the primary number, but total includes reserved chips that are committed to pots and might not come back. These 10 options explore different philosophies: available-first, visual bars, budget-centric, and more. Each option shows the Home badge, Turn header, and Hand card snippet.

Option 01

Available-First

The big number is Available — what you can actually bet. Total shown small underneath as context. Reserved is implicit (total minus available). Clean and honest.

Option 03

Visual Stack Bar

A proportional bar shows how your stack is allocated. Gold = available, amber = in pots, empty = opponent's share of the 4000. Instant visual read of relative position.

03Visual Stack BarProportional bar. Gold = available, dark gold = in pots. Instant visual of stack health and allocation.
